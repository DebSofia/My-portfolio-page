I was born in Switzerland, in 1993, but soon came to Portugal, to live with my grandparents. I have a younger sister and a younger brother. Since I was younger I always enjoyed painting and playing with colors.

I’ve always enjoyed art,history and geography classes, but my academic journey didn’t led to me study any of that subjects. Instead, I’ve studied Languages and Business Relations, at Aveiro University. There, I had my first contact with HTML, CSS, DreamWeaver, etc. I remember enjoying learning those tool, but I didn’t think about it for some years, until 2021.

In 2021, I tried learning CSS, HTML and JavaScript with FreecodeCamp and Udemy. I thought that, since I always enjoyed painting and playing with colors, and design is something that I am very passionate about, I got my certification in Graphic Design. This passion to learn just gave me more motivation to further my knowledge and started to attending SheCodes workshops, where I found a complement for my passion, WebDesign & FrontEndDevelopment.
